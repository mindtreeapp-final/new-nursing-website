About This Course

An Internationally Qualified Nurse (IQN) is a nurse who has obtained their nursing qualification and professional registration in one country and is applying for recognition and registration to practice nursing in another country.

IQNs must meet regulatory standards, including qualification assessment, competency requirements, and language proficiency, as set by the nursing council of the destination country.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand international nursing standards and requirements
  • Prepare necessary documentation for qualification assessment
  • Learn about different healthcare systems worldwide
  • Develop skills for international nursing practice
  • Navigate the qualification recognition process
  • Meet competency standards for various countries
  • Build professional portfolio for international applications
